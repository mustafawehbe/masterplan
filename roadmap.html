Fernando Belasteguín Partnership Strategic · Handle Carefully
Belasteguín — 16 years world #1, the GOAT of padel — is the most powerful brand association available in this sport globally. His name opens doors in Spain, Latin America, the GCC, and anywhere padel is growing. This partnership, done right, is a differentiator no competitor can replicate.
Recommended Structure
  • Avoid: 5% of total revenue — too expensive at scale (3.4M AED/yr by Year 3)
  • Best option: 3–5% equity stake — aligns incentives, impresses investors
  • Alternative: flat $200K/yr + 1% of club revenue only
  • His name on the club + academy, not all verticals
  • Check HEAD racket deal before any equipment conversation
What He Brings
  • Global padel credibility — instant legitimacy
  • Present at Dubai opening → massive PR + media
  • Access to his professional network (tours, sponsors)
  • Branding: "Belasteguín Academy at [Club Name]"
  • Attracts top coaches trained under him

Padel Equipment Shop Strategy Competing with NOX · Babolat · Wilson · Adidas
The market is crowded but none of the existing brands own a club network. Equipment sold inside our clubs to members who identify with the brand — 65%+ gross margin with no retailer taking a cut. The brand is born inside the club, not on a shelf.
Positioning
  • Premium, club-native — not mass market
  • Identity-driven like Lululemon or On Running
  • Padel-only, not tennis crossover
  • Bela signature line if partnership includes equipment rights
  • UAE / GCC designed — Arabic branding variant
Year 1 Product Range
  • 3 racket SKUs: Entry (600 AED) · Pro (900 AED) · Signature (1,400 AED)
  • Padel bag + accessories
  • Club apparel: polo, shorts, cap, socks
  • Sold in-club + DTC online + future retail
  • Manufactured in Spain / Portugal (premium origin)
