Business Plan

Vertically integrated padel ecosystem — clubs, franchise, app, equipment, and tournaments.

Executive Summary The Pitch
We are building the world's first vertically integrated padel company — owning every touchpoint of the player's journey from the moment they step on a court to the racket in their hand. Padel is the fastest-growing sport on earth: 35 million players, a $2B global market expanding at 15% CAGR, and no dominant brand that owns the full stack. Our model combines premium club operations, a scalable franchise network, a proprietary digital platform (booking, coaching, matchmaking, tournaments), a padel equipment brand, and a professional tournament circuit — creating compounding revenue streams and a moat that no single-vertical competitor can replicate.
The Problem Fragmentation

Padel is exploding globally but the ecosystem is completely fragmented:

  • → Club operators run on generic booking tools with no brand identity or tech stack
  • → Players have no unified platform for booking, finding partners, coaching, or competing
  • → Equipment brands are tennis-first; no pure-play padel brand at scale
  • → No franchise model exists to rapidly expand quality clubs globally
  • → Tournaments are siloed with no link to club or digital communities
The Solution Full Stack Padel

Own every layer of the padel stack — no competitor can replicate the full vertical:

  • ✓ Clubs — premium-designed padel facilities as the physical anchor
  • ✓ Franchise — license the model globally for rapid, capital-light scaling
  • ✓ App — booking, coaching, matchmaking, and live tournament tracking
  • ✓ Equipment — proprietary racket and apparel brand built for serious players
  • ✓ Tournaments — owned circuit creating content, community, and sponsorship revenue

Go-to-Market Strategy 3-Phase Rollout
Phase 1 — Anchor (Year 1)
  • Launch flagship club in Dubai (UAE)
  • Build and launch the app (iOS + Android)
  • Launch equipment brand — 3 racket SKUs + apparel
  • Run first regional tournament circuit
  • Document the operating playbook
Phase 2 — Franchise (Year 2)
  • Launch franchise program with pilot partners
  • Expand to 5 owned + 10 franchised clubs
  • Scale app to 25K+ active users
  • Establish equipment distribution in key markets
  • Partner with Premier Padel / regional tours
Phase 3 — Scale (Year 3+)
  • 50+ locations across MENA, Europe, Americas
  • App as standalone B2B SaaS for non-franchise clubs
  • Equipment brand in global retail + online
  • Launch owned professional tournament circuit
  • Pursue Series A / strategic investment
Why Dubai First
Dubai is the ideal launch market: high-income, sports-obsessed population; government support for sports infrastructure; zero corporate tax; gateway to $1.5T GCC market; strong appetite for premium lifestyle brands; and padel is already the fastest-growing sport in the UAE with demand significantly exceeding supply. The flagship becomes our proof-of-concept, media engine, and franchise showcase.

The Moat
Vertical integration is the moat. Each layer reinforces the others: clubs drive app users → app drives tournament participation → tournaments drive equipment sales → equipment drives club loyalty → clubs attract franchise partners. No competitor owns more than one layer. By the time a single-vertical competitor tries to expand, we have brand, data, and community across all five.
